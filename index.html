Our website is an immersive platform designed to unearth the captivating history and vibrant culture of Bangalore's lesser-known and extraordinary locales.Our mission is to:

  • Preserve Heritage
  • Educate & Inform
  • Foster Appreciation
  • Digital Accessibility
  • Promoting Tourism

Here are our suggestion for you to explore

Founded in 2012 by Subodh Sankar and Lalitha Lakshmi, the bookstore revives regional literature, fostering a vibrant culture of reading and storytelling among the youth with passion and innovation.

Atta Galatta

Step into the world of the National Gallery of Modern Art, a haven where art, life, and human spirit intertwine. Delight, understand, and connect deeper with the mesmerizing dance of modern expressions!

National Gallery of Modern Art

Discover the 17th-century Gudibande Fort, crafted by Byre Gowda of the Tuluva dynasty! A marvel with seven interconnected floors, echoing the grandeur of Madhugiri fort, awaits your awe and wonder!

Gudibanda Fort

IME’s vision, passionately upheld by first director Manasi Prasad, is a vibrant dance of preserving and celebrating India’s rich musical tapestry, immersing the youth in its diverse, historic melodic wonders!

Indian Music Experience Musuem

Visit the 200-year-old majestic temple of Lord Malleshwara, a Dravidian architectural gem! Still resplendent and bustling with devotees, and neighbored by the renowned Nandi Teertha, it’s a spiritual and cultural oasis!

Kaadu Malleshwara Temple

Despite urban sprawl, Turahalli's lush embrace offers solace to bustling Bangalore. Amidst congestion and construction, this green sanctuary stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of the ‘Garden City’!

Turahalli Forest
